About Me

I’m a qualified Mechanical Engineer with over 7 years of academic training (HND, PGCert, and MEng) and over 4 years of industry experience including both professional and practical. My background spans mechanical design, prototyping, and process optimisation — with a strong focus on turning ideas into practical, working solutions.

I’m passionate about research and development, problem-solving, and helping businesses improve efficiency through smart engineering solutions. From designing jigs and fixtures to developing new concepts, I bring a mix of technical expertise and hands-on creativity to every project.
