Tractor Toolbox

A durable, precision-engineered storage solution for the iconic Massey Ferguson 135.

Overview

Designed to complement the timeless look of the Massey Ferguson 135, this toolbox provides secure, weather-resistant storage without compromising the tractor’s aesthetic. Each unit is built in-house to exacting standards and can be customised to suit your needs.

Specifications

Material: Structural Steel
Finish: TBC
Mounting: Direct fit for MF135 weight frame- brackets and bolts provided.
Weight: ~15kg
Dimensions: 400mm x 300mm x 300mm
Manufactured: In-house by ShapeShift Engineering

Design & Engineering

This design combines functionality with respect for vintage machinery. Each toolbox is cut, welded, and finished using precision fabrication techniques to ensure reliability in the field. The internal design allows for both tool organization and protection from the elements. The design allows space for two orgional weights along with the tolbox.

The toolbox has enough room to store common tools needed daily on the farm and then some imcludng sections for handtools, fasteners and larger tools.
