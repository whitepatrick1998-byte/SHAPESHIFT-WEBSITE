Project Two - EVO Coil Pack Cover

About Project Two

The client required a custom replacment solution for his car.

The customer required a trasparent version of the standard part.

Solution

Reverse engineering and CAD modelling was used to allow me to quote for a vaccum forming solution involving a high temperature thermoplastic alternative.

Outcome

The solution required the development of multiple prototype parts to match the original component while ensuring consistent physical transparency. The CAD file was provided to the client, enabling them to explore alternative solutions as needed.
